## Notes

- Lavalink supports plugins through jars in the `plugins` directory or through dependency declarations in `application.yml`.
- youtube-source replaces Lavalink's deprecated built-in YouTube source.
- LavaSrc exposes multiple music service sources, but many require credentials or account cookies.
- yt-dlp provides the broad extractor coverage used for hundreds of additional URL sources.
